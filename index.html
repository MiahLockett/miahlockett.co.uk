Ever wanted to own your own Café, unlock rewards and discover a new persona?

Toasty Bytes is a new 2D pixel game, similar to your favourites; Stardew Valley and Good Coffee, Great Coffee.

From upgrading your Café to discovering family secret recipes, playing Toasty Bytes is the perfect way to spend your evening after a busy day, to relax and unwind. No need to spend hours grinding a game to finally enjoy it, with Toasty Bytes you enjoy it from the start!

Includes features such as buying ingredients, unlocking new recipes through gameplay, upgrading coffee beans, making friendships with the local people, working through rush hours to keep customers happy, maintaining a good Café score — and so much more!
